Welcome to Sperrmüll Bonn

This application allows you to see Sperrmüll collection events on a map based on your location and selected dates.

How to use:

  1. Click the "Continue" button below to go to the input form
  2. Enter your location or use your current location
  3. Select a date or a range of dates
  4. Submit the form to see Sperrmüll collection events on the map
  5. Click on pins to see information about each collection event
